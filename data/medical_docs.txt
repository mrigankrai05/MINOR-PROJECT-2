Low hemoglobin may indicate anemia which can cause fatigue and weakness.

Normal hemoglobin range for adult males is 13 to 17 g/dL.

High WBC count may indicate infection or inflammation.

Normal WBC range is between 4 and 10 x1000/µL.

Platelet count between 150 and 450 is considered normal.

Low platelets may cause bleeding problems.

High cholesterol increases risk of heart disease.

Normal RBC count ranges between 4.5 and 5.5 million cells per microliter.
